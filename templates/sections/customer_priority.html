Know Your Customer

Know your customer's needs and priorities based on the consumer results with Lowest, Low-Average, High-Average, or Highest spending power.

1. Highest

Customers with the highest spending power. They are the most loyal customers and have the highest purchasing power.

2. High-Average

Customers with high average spending power. They are loyal customers and have a high purchasing power.

3. Low-Average

Customers with low average spending power. They are maybe loyal and have a low purchasing power.

4. Lowest

Customers with the lowest spending power. They are not loyal customers and have the lowest purchasing power.
